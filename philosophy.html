Philosophy


Vivanti Hair Studio, Inc. offers exceptional hair care services that leave you feeling pampered, rejuvenated and renewed. Nestled in the downtown Silver Spring area, Vivanti Hair Studio has been in business for over thirty (30) years. We specialize in the latest hair trends from cuts, colors, weaves and natural hair care services tailored to fit your needs. This is the place where you can come to relax and discover your own personal sense of style and comfort.

Vivanti Hair Studio has grown steadily over the years, under the leadership of excellent talents. We have ongoing training in the newest trends and techniques available, as well as an extensive line of professional hair care products.
